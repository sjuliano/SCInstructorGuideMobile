Being Well Topics

A COMPLEX MACHINE

  • Process the opening video, and provide additional examples pointing to the body and brain’s complexity.
  • Introduce the idea of being careful with our amazing body and brains.

OUR ENGINEERED BODY

  • Show the complexity of our body’s many systems.
  • Look inside of the complex, engineered body.

OUR SENSITIVE BRAIN

  • Continue to promote a sense of awe about the brain’s complexity.
  • Introduce the idea and caution that the brain is, by nature, sensitive, and must be kept in good condition and cared for.

PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE

  • Provide human examples of people effectively maintaining their brains and bodies.
  • Show that it’s important to maintain your mind and body (just as you would your vehicle) in order for it to run the best it can and in order to maximize readiness.

THE CHEMICAL BRAIN

  • Describe how all substances (not just alcohol and drugs) affect our body’s chemistry.
  • Describes some ways that alcohol and drugs can affect our chemistry in some surprising ways—not just through impairment or hangovers.
Play Note

CHOICES AS MAINTENANCE

  • Define the critical areas of the brain’s functions that can be impacted negatively by high-risk use.
  • Describe what happens to those brain functions, in the short and long term.
  • Describe what happens to other body functions as a result of alcohol and drug use.

STANDARD DRINKS

  • Restate the importance of establishing protective standards for the body and brain.
  • State what a standard drink is, and list standard drink amounts for beer, wine, and liquor.

LOW-RISK GUIDELINES

  • State the low-risk guidelines.
  • Describe a common “trap” associated with counting drinks that are not one standard drink.

FAMILY HISTORY

  • Show a dramatic interpretation of the biological basis of addiction and alcoholism using adoption research.

TOLERANCE IS A LIABILITY

  • Explain the dangers of a high tolerance to pain.
  • Describe how a high tolerance to alcohol can increase one’s risk for problems.
  • Describe how a high tolerance to alcohol can increase the likelihood that a person will become dependent.
